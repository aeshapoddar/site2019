“COME GET ILLUMINATED” TEDxYouth@JNS
Conversations today, Revolutions tomorrow.

"Never underestimate the valuable and important difference you make in every life you touch. For the impact you make today has a powerful rippling effect on every tomorrow."

At TEDxYouth@JNS, our aim is to amplify the voices of the community and bring together the best minds to create a better tomorrow, for ourselves and for the world. With the third edition of our TEDx event, we hope to take action and leave a resonating impact on this modern world! Abiding by our schoolâ€™s motto, "Knowledge is strength supreme", we aim to share the ideas that are worth sharing and support change and arouse curiosity.

TEDxYouth@JNS aims to share this knowledge through the visionary ideas and wisdom of our numerous speakers. TEDxYouth@JNS will be held on 14th August, 2020 at 5p.m. . Get ready to embark on an enlightening and edifying experience from the comfort of your homes! See the change, Be the change.

Timeline

4th April, 2020
The TEDxYouth@JNS license was applied for by Primary Organizer, Rahael Braganza and Team Heads for the year 2020 were formally finalized.

4th June, 2020
TEDx issued the license for the TEDxYouth@JNS Event, 2020 and Rahael Braganza became the official Primary Organizer and Licensee of the event with Ms. Brinda Bose as Co-organizer.

6th June, 2020
Commencement of the Final Interviews to select the team members to constitute the Organizing Committee, 2020. Interviews were conducted by the Primary Organizer and Licensee, Team Heads and Members of the Advisory Committee of TEDxYouth@JNS.

10th June, 2020
The Organizing Committee of TEDxYouth@JNS 2020 was officiated and approved by the Principal and Teachers in charge. Introductory proceedings had commenced.

19th June, 2020
Social Media promotions of the event had started off with introductory posts on all three of our pages - Facebook, Instagram and Twitter and promotions were under way by word of mouth as well.

23rd June, 2020
The creation of a database of prospective speakers began to take shape. The work on the website and process of building social media presence was also under development.

29th June, 2020
The official decision of taking TEDxYouth@JNS 2020 online due to the coronavirus pandemic was made and informed. The date for the event was also set to the 14th of August, 2020. The deliberation of speakers was an on going process and the speaker guide was being formed. The team was also exploring myriad technological avenues in order to ascertain yet another successful event despite the current challenges.

4/5/19
An introductory meeting was conducted with the entire newly formed Organizing Committee and team members were allotted their respective teams.

22/5/19
BTS shooting and videos of the team in work commenced.

24/5/19
Finalising of speakers had begun. Distribution of tickets was underway.

19/6/19
Extensive social media promotions, especially on twitter, had started.

2/7/19
Practice runs for the event had begun. The team was procuring all materials required from school and was gearing up for event day.

5/7/19
Event day.
TEDxYouth@JNS hosted it's second rendition which was even more successful than the previous one. Everything fell into place and the event progressed seamlessly. It was an exemplary team effort.

22/7/19
Post event work was underway. 'Thank you' cards were being created to be distributed to the school management and support staff for their constant assistance.

28/8/19
Talks from the event were uploaded to TED and on YouTube as well. The playlists were made accessible and the keyword, "TEDxYouth@JNS" was publicly searchable.

 7/10/19
 Felicitation Ceremony was held to recognise the effort and hardwork put in by all team members to ascertain a successful event.

September 8th, 2018
The first TEDxYouth@JNS event was launched. 100 viewers + 2 viewing parties! An event organized solely by the students of Jamnabai Narsee School.
