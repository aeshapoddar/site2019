2020

Avani Dalal

“You can only hope for a change if you have sown the seeds of change.” With that thought in mind, she has been striving hard to make changes at grass-root levels since 2015 as the co-founder trustee of Spark-A-Change Foundation, a 5 year old Mumbai-based NGO registered as a Charitable Trust under the Bombay Public Trust Act,1950. She enjoys working with the SAC core team for generating awareness about campaigns, curating SAC curriculum, fund-raising and provide overall development with after-school and in-school programmes to 520 underprivileged children with the help of 60 committed volunteers.

Captain Rishabh Kapur

Captain Rishabh Kapur is one of the many unsung heroes that have helped unite thousands of families in india by being part of the Vande Bharat Mission during the Corona Virus Pandemic. He also executed operation RAHAT, which was the most complex evacuation ever in Indian Aviation during the Yemen Crisis. When it comes to serving the nation, even the sky is not the limit for him.

Brian Tellis

Brian Tellis is an unparalled pioneer in the events and entertainment industry with over 3 decades of experience .He has co-founded and served as the President of Fountainhead Promotions and Events Pvt Ltd . Brian is also an accomplished emcee,musician and has several other accomplishments under his belt .We can't wait to see what Brian has in store for us and we \ are looking forward to his talk on the 14th Of August at TEDxYouth@JNS 2020!

Kartik Desai

Kartik, born in Delhi and raised in Geneva and New York, leads Asha Impact, an impact investing and policy advocacy platform for major Indian business leaders, UHNIs and philanthropists to invest in scalable, for-profit social enterprises. A Wharton and Columbia University graduate who started his career with Merrill Lynch, he is considered a pioneer of the impact financing ecosystem in India, having worked at the two largest impact funds (Lok Capital & Aavishkaar), leading India’s first virtual fund for social sector startups, judging a reality TV show on impact investing, launching a co-working and events space for underprivileged youth, representing 40+ major investors as board member of the industry association, writing several op-eds, advising the government on development policy issues, and being selected as a ‘Champions of Change’ by the PMO and Niti Aayog. Kartik will talk about how the youth of India can create the impact they want to see in the country. We can assure you that the ROI of your time on the 14th of August will be extremely high!

Shivani Khetan

Shivani, a certified life coach from Symbiosis Boston is an exemplary business professional with over 8 years of rich extensive experience. She is a certified master practitioner of NLP (Neuro Linguistic Programming), has completed certificate course in guidance and counselling orientation. Mrs. Shivani is the founder of the renowned “Expresso Talk”, an organization which provides life and relationship counselling and gives succour and strength to those who have been emotionally shattered.

Dawn Cordo

Dawn Cordo is a professional musician who worked on several projects in the music industry in Mumbai before studying at the Berklee College of Music in Boston. After completing her studies she returned to Mumbai to work on her music education program called The Gleehive Music Program, to help hone the skills of the next generation. Dawn also continues her professional work, as music director, arranger and composer for varying projects, from theatre and the performing arts to studio work.

Ajay Verma

Ajay Verma. An advertising professional, A serial entrepreneur and a vintage motorcycle collector. He has held leadership roles in agencies like DraftFCB, Rediffusion Y&R, Contract & Percept H and led many successful brands. He has had ups and downs throughout. Currently he is a Managing Partner with Enormous Brands – A leading Independent adverting agency in India. He shares the same sentiments of Steve Forbes, “Your brand is the single most important investment you can make in your business.”
